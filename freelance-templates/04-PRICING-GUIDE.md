## 1. CARA MENENTUKAN HARGA

### Formula Dasar:

```
TARGET MONTHLY INCOME
÷ Workable Hours per Month
× Complexity Multiplier
+ Business Expenses (20%)
= YOUR HOURLY RATE
```

### Contoh Perhitungan:

| Variable | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Target Monthly Income | $3,000 |
| Workable Hours (25 hrs/week) | 100 hours |
| Complexity Multiplier | 1.2x |
| Business Expenses (20%) | $600 |
| **Hourly Rate** | **$36** |

### Perbandingan Rate Global:

| Level | Hourly Rate | Monthly (Part-time) | Monthly (Full-time) |
|-------|-------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Beginner | $15-25 | $1,500-2,500 | $3,000-5,000 |
| Intermediate | $30-50 | $3,000-5,000 | $6,000-10,000 |

| Premium | $100-200+ | $10,000-20,000+ | $20,000-40,000+ |

## 2. PRICING MODELS

### Model 1: Hourly Rate ⏰
**Best for:** Project yang scope-nya belum jelas, ongoing work

| Kelebihan | Kekurangan |
|-----------|------------|
| Flexibility | Client bisa "scope creep" |
| Easy to start | Income capped by hours |
| Lower risk | Client prefer fixed price |

**Contoh:**
```
Junior Developer: $20-35/hour
Mid Developer: $40-75/hour
Senior Developer: $80-150/hour
```

---

### Model 2: Fixed Price (Project-Based) 📦
**Best for:** Clear deliverables, defined scope

**Rumus:**
```
Estimated Hours × Hourly Rate × 1.3 (buffer)
= Fixed Price
```

**Contoh:**
```
Website Development:
- 40 hours × $50/hr × 1.3 = $2,600
- Offer as: $2,500 (rounded)
```

**Kelebihan:**
- Predictable income
- Client loves certainty
- Can overdeliver without cost

**Kekurangan:**
- Scope creep = lose money
- Unforeseen issues = lose money

---

### Model 3: Retainer (Monthly) 📅
**Best for:** Ongoing clients, steady income

**Struktur:**
```
Monthly Retainer = Weekly Hours × Hourly Rate × 4
+ Premium (10-20% for availability guarantee)
```

**Contoh:**
```
10 hours/week × $50/hr × 4 weeks = $2,000
+ Retainer premium (15%) = $2,300/month
```

**Benefits:**
- Predictable monthly income
- Priority access
- Relationship building

---

### Model 4: Value-Based Pricing 💎
**Best for:** High-ticket services, proven ROI

**Rumus:**
```
Price = Value Delivered to Client × Percentage You Want
```

**Contoh:**
```
Client makes $100,000/year from your work
You want 10% of that value
Price = $10,000 (for 1 year)
```

## 5. HOW TO RAISE YOUR RATES

### Strategi #1: Gradual Increase
```
Every 6 months: Raise rates by 10-15%
Exception: Exceptional performance = immediate raise
```

### Strategi #2: Raise for New Clients Only
1. Keep existing clients at old rate for 3 months
2. Announce rate increase
3. New clients = new rate

### Strategi #3: Value-Based Switch
1. Calculate the value you deliver
2. Price based on value, not time
3. Justify with ROI metrics

### Script untuk Rate Increase:
```
"Hi [Client Name],

I hope you're happy with our work together!

I wanted to let you know that starting [date], 
my rates will be increasing to $[new rate].

For your next project/booked hours, you'll lock in 
the current rate of $[old rate] if we can confirm 
before [deadline].

This increase reflects [reason - experience, demand, 
inflation] and allows me to continue providing 
top-quality work.

Let me know if you have any questions!

Best,
[Your Name]"
```

## 7. COMMON PRICING MISTAKES TO AVOID

| ❌ Mistake | ✅ Solution |
|-----------|------------|
| Underpricing untuk dapat project | Price for value, not survival |
| Same price untuk semua client | Adjust based on complexity |
| Never raising rates | Annual review + inflation |
| Discounting terlalu cepat | Offer value-add instead |
| Charging per hour only | Mix with fixed/variable models |
| Ignoring platform fees | Factor in all costs |
| Not tracking time | Know your real profitability |

## 💡 PRICING CHEAT SHEET:
## ============================================
1. Start higher than you think (room to discount)

7. Premium clients = premium pricing
