# 📬 COVER LETTER & PITCH TEMPLATES

## ============================================
## TEMPLATE 1: UPWORK COVER LETTER
## ============================================

```
Hi [Client Name],

I came across your project [Project Title] and I'm excited about the opportunity 
to help you [main goal/outcome].

With [X] years of experience in [your niche], I've helped clients achieve:

✓ [Specific Result 1 - use numbers]
✓ [Specific Result 2 - use numbers]  
✓ [Specific Result 3 - use numbers]

For example, I recently [relevant accomplishment that matches their needs]. 
This resulted in [quantifiable impact].

Here's my approach for your project:

1. [Step 1 - addressing their specific need]
2. [Step 2 - addressing their specific concern]
3. [Step 3 - showing additional value]

I'm available to start immediately and can [specific timeline commitment].

To ensure we're a good fit, I have a few questions:
• [Question 1]
• [Question 2]

Looking forward to discussing this further!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Portfolio Link]
```

## ============================================
## 💡 COVER LETTER TIPS:
## ============================================
1. ✅ Personalize EVERY message - no generic templates
2. ✅ Lead with client benefit, not your credentials
3. ✅ Use numbers wherever possible (metrics, results)
4. ✅ Show you've read their job description
5. ✅ Ask ONE relevant question (engagement)
6. ✅ Keep it scannable (short paragraphs, bullets)
7. ✅ End with clear CTA (call to action)
8. ❌ Don't start with "I am writing to..."
9. ❌ Don't list everything you've ever done
10. ❌ Don't be too salesy or desperate
