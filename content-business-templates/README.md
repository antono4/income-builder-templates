## 🚀 CARA MEMULAI CONTENT BUSINESS

### Phase 1: Foundation (Minggu 1-2)
1. ✅ Pilih 1 platform utama (YouTube atau Blog)
2. ✅ Tentukan 3-5 content pillars
3. ✅ Setup accounts & basic branding
4. ✅ Buat 1 piece content per hari

### Phase 2: Consistency (Minggu 3-8)
1. 📅 Publish 3-5x per minggu
2. 📧 Mulai email newsletter
3. 📊 Analisis metrics mingguan
4. 🔄 Repurpose content ke multiple platforms

### Phase 3: Monetization (Bulan 3-6)
1. 💵 Affiliate marketing
2. 📦 Digital products
3. 🎥 Sponsored content
4. 💼 Services dari audience
