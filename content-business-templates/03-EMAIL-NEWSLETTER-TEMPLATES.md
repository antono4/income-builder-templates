## 4. RE-ENGAGEMENT EMAILS

## 5. SUBJECT LINE FORMULAS

### CURIOSITY DRIVEN
```
- "I made [X] last week doing this..."
- "The mistake nobody talks about..."
- "What [famous person] taught me about [topic]"
- "I was wrong about [common belief]"
- "Nobody talks about [X]..."
```

### NUMBER BASED
```
- "[X] things I wish I knew about [topic]"
- "[X] ways to [achieve result]"
- "[X] mistakes that cost me $[X]"
- "[X] lessons from [X] years of [experience]"
```

### QUESTION BASED
```
- "Ready to [benefit]?"
- "Are you making this mistake?"
- "What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?"
- "Is [X] worth it?"
```

### URGENCY/SCARCITY
```
- "Last chance: [deadline/limited spots]"
- "[X] hours left to [action]"
- "⚠️ [Important update/change]"
- "Ends tonight: [offer/event]"
```

### PERSONAL/CASUAL
```
- "Quick thought on [topic]..."
- "Just for you: [exclusive content]"
- "A story about [topic]..."
- "My [X] cents on [topic]"
```

### BENEFIT DRIVEN
```
- "How to [achieve result] in [timeframe]"
- "The fastest way to [benefit]"
- "Get [specific result] without [pain point]"
- "Finally, a [solution] that actually works"
```

## ✅ EMAIL CHECKLIST

### Before Sending:
- [ ] Subject line A/B tested
- [ ] Preview text optimized
- [ ] Personalization token working
- [ ] Links tested (all working)
- [ ] Images loading properly
- [ ] Mobile responsive
- [ ] No spelling/grammar errors
- [ ] CTA is clear

### After Sending:
- [ ] Monitor open rates (within 2 hours)
- [ ] Check for bounce backs
- [ ] Respond to replies
- [ ] Note what worked for next time

## ============================================
## 💡 EMAIL MARKETING TIPS:
## ============================================
1. ✅ Write subject lines last (after writing the email)
2. ✅ Personalize when possible ([First Name] goes a long way)
3. ✅ Keep emails short (aim for 200-300 words)
4. ✅ Use 1 CTA per email
5. ✅ Reply to every response (builds relationship)
6. ✅ Test and optimize constantly
7. ❌ Don't buy email lists
8. ❌ Don't spam or oversell
9. ❌ Don't ignore unsubscribes
10. ❌ Don't send inconsistent schedules
