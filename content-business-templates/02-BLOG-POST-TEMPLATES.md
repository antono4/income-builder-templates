- [ ] Primary keyword in: Title, URL, H1, first paragraph

- [ ] Image alt texts
- [ ] Internal links (2-3 minimum)

- [ ] Table of contents (for long posts)
- [ ] Featured image ready
- [ ] FAQ section

### After Publishing:
- [ ] Submit to Google Search Console
- [ ] Share on social media
- [ ] Add to newsletter
- [ ] Schedule for Pinterest/other platforms
- [ ] Monitor for comments

## ============================================
## 💡 BLOGGING SUCCESS TIPS:
## ============================================
1. ✅ Write for humans first, SEO second
2. ✅ Aim for 1,500+ words minimum for SEO
3. ✅ Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
4. ✅ Include visuals (images, screenshots, videos)
5. ✅ Update old posts regularly
6. ✅ Promote on social media after publishing
7. ✅ Respond to all comments
8. ❌ Don't keyword stuff
9. ❌ Don't copy what others are writing
10. ❌ Don't publish without editing
