---

### 📊 CONTENT FUNNEL STRATEGY

```
AWARENESS (Top of Funnel - TOFU)
├── Tujuannya: Menarik perhatian, brand awareness
├── Content Type: Blog posts, social media, videos
├── Contoh: Tips, how-to basics, industry news
└── CTA: Follow, subscribe

CONSIDERATION (Middle of Funnel - MOFU)
├── Tujuannya: Educate, build trust
├── Content Type: Case studies, guides, webinars
├── Contoh: Comparisons, in-depth tutorials
└── CTA: Download ebook, email list

DECISION (Bottom of Funnel - BOFU)
├── Tujuannya: Convert, close sale
├── Content Type: Demos, testimonials, offers
├── Contoh: Product reviews, pricing, consultations
└── CTA: Buy, hire, subscribe
```

---

### 📊 CONTENT PRODUCTION EFFORT MATRIX

```
HIGH IMPACT + LOW EFFORT = QUICK WINS (Do First!)
├── Social media comments
├── Story responses
├── Repurposed content
├── User-generated content shares
└── Quick tips posts

HIGH IMPACT + HIGH EFFORT = MAJOR PROJECTS (Schedule)
├── YouTube videos
├── Long-form blog posts
├── Webinars
├── Courses
└── Ebooks

LOW IMPACT + LOW EFFORT = FILLERS (Batch)
├── Quotes
├── Memes
├── Generic tips
└── Engagement comments

LOW IMPACT + HIGH EFFORT = ELIMINATE (Don't Do)
├── Generic advice
├── Content without clear goal
└── Content not optimized for platform
```

## 💡 CONTENT PRODUCTION TIPS

### Batch Production Workflow:
1. **Batch Create:** Create content in batches (e.g., create all graphics for week on Tuesday)
2. **Schedule Ahead:** Use scheduling tools (Later, Buffer, Hootsuite)
3. **Repurpose:** Turn 1 YouTube video into 10+ pieces
4. **Templates:** Use templates for consistent branding
5. **AI Assistance:** Use AI for drafting, then humanize

### Repurposing Blueprint:
```
1 YouTube Video =
├── 1 Blog Post (transcription + expansion)
├── 3 Instagram Posts (key points)
├── 5 Twitter Threads (takeaways)
├── 1 LinkedIn Article (professional angle)
├── 7 Instagram Stories (teasers)
├── 3 YouTube Shorts (best moments)
├── 1 Email Newsletter (summary + link)
└── 1 Podcast Episode (deeper discussion)
```
