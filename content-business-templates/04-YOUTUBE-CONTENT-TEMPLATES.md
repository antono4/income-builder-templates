## 1. YOUTUBE VIDEO TEMPLATES

### 🎓 TUTORIAL/HOW-TO VIDEO

```markdown
# 🎓 HOW-TO VIDEO SCRIPT

**Title:** How to [ACHIEVE OUTCOME] in [TIMEFRAME]
**Duration:** 10-15 minutes
**Script Length:** ~1,500-2,000 words

---

## PRE-SHOOT CHECKLIST
- [ ] Camera & lighting set up
- [ ] Microphone tested
- [ ] B-roll footage prepared
- [ ] Props/demo materials ready
- [ ] Script rehearsed

---

## SCRIPT:

### 🎬 HOOK (0:00 - 0:30)

[Look directly at camera with energy]

"Yo, what's up everyone! If you're watching this video, you're probably 
looking to [achieve outcome]. Well, you're in the right place because 
today I'm going to show you exactly how to do that."

[Smile/energetic transition]

"By the end of this video, you're going to know [specific things they'll learn]. 
Let's dive right in!"

---

### 📝 INTRODUCTION (0:30 - 1:30)

"My name is [Name] and I help people [benefit]. I've been doing this for 
[X years/months] and today I want to share with you everything I know."

"Before we start, make sure you subscribe and hit that bell icon because 
I post videos like this every [frequency] and you don't want to miss them."

[Quick promotional reminder]

"Alright, let's get into it."

---

### 💡 MAIN CONTENT (1:30 - 12:00)

**SECTION 1: [Topic Name] (1:30 - 4:00)**

"So first, let's talk about [topic]."

[Explain concept clearly, use examples]

"The key thing to remember is: [most important point]."

[Transition to next section]

"Now that you understand [Topic 1], let's move on to..."

---

**SECTION 2: [Topic Name] (4:00 - 7:00)**

"Here's where things get interesting."

[Continue with educational content]

"Pro tip: [Advanced insight]."

[Use B-roll footage here if available]

"Many beginners make the mistake of [common mistake]. Don't do that. 
Instead, do [correct approach]."

[Transition]

"Let's keep going..."

---

**SECTION 3: [Topic Name] (7:00 - 10:00)**

"This is where most people mess up."

[Share common pitfalls]

"Here's a better approach: [solution]."

[Live demonstration if applicable]

[Do demonstration on screen]

"As you can see, it's pretty straightforward once you know how."

---

**SECTION 4: [Final Tips/Advanced] (10:00 - 12:00)**

"Before we wrap up, let me share a few advanced tips."

1. [Tip 1 with brief explanation]
2. [Tip 2 with brief explanation]
3. [Tip 3 with brief explanation]

"These aren't required, but if you want to take your [skill/results] to 
the next level, definitely implement these."

---

### 🎯 CONCLUSION (12:00 - 13:00)

"Alright, let's wrap this up."

"Today we covered:
1. [Key point 1]
2. [Key point 2]
3. [Key point 3]"

"If you implement even just a few of these strategies, I promise you're 
going to see [desired outcome]."

**Call to Action:**

"If you found value in this video:
✅ SMASH that like button
✅ Subscribe if you haven't
✅ Hit the bell icon
✅ Drop a comment below with [question/topic for next video]"

[Engagement question]

"I read every comment and I'd love to hear your thoughts."

---

### 👋 OUTRO (13:00 - 13:30)

"Thanks for watching! I'll see you in the next one."

[Brief outro card with related video suggestion]

---

## EDITOR NOTES:
- Add [text overlays] at timestamps
- Music: [Type] at [volume level]
- B-roll: [Description] at sections [X]
- Graphics: [Callouts/text] at [timestamps]
```

### 📖 STORY/OPINION VIDEO

```markdown
# 📖 STORY/OPINION VIDEO SCRIPT

**Title:** I [DID/LEARNED/REALIZED] [SOMETHING INTERESTING]
**Duration:** 8-12 minutes
**Tone:** Conversational, personal, engaging

---

## SCRIPT:

### 🎬 HOOK (0:00 - 0:20)

"So a few [days/weeks/months] ago, something happened that changed 
the way I think about [topic]."

OR

"I'm going to tell you a story about the time when [interesting situation]."

[Pause for effect]

"And honestly, I still think about it almost every day."

---

### 📝 SETTING THE SCENE (0:20 - 1:30)

"Here's what happened:"

[Set up the context]

"At the time, I was [situation/context]. I remember feeling 
[emotion] because [reason]."

"The reason I'm sharing this story is because [connect to larger lesson]."

---

### 📖 THE STORY (1:30 - 8:00)

**The Beginning:**
"So it started when [setup the situation]..."

[Tell the story chronologically with good pacing]

**The Challenge:**
"The problem was, [explain the problem/challenge]."

"I tried [first approach], but [why it didn't work]."

**The Turning Point:**
"Then something clicked."

[This is where the key insight/moment happens]

"I realized that [the key realization]."

**The Resolution:**
"After that, I [what you did differently]."

"And the result was [outcome/change]."

---

### 💭 LESSONS & TAKEAWAYS (8:00 - 10:00)

"So what did I learn from all this?"

1. **[Lesson 1]**
   → [Brief explanation and why it matters]

2. **[Lesson 2]**
   → [Brief explanation and why it matters]

3. **[Lesson 3]**
   → [Brief explanation and why it matters]

"The biggest thing I took away from this experience was: [main insight]."

---

### 🎯 CONCLUSION (10:00 - 11:00)

"If you're going through something similar, I want you to know that [reassurance]."

"And if you take one thing from this video, let it be this: [single key message]."

**CTA:**
"If this resonated with you, smash that like button and let me know in 
the comments. Have you ever had a similar experience?"

[Engagement prompt]

"Subscribe if you want more videos like this. I'll see you next time."

---

### 👋 OUTRO

[Related video suggestion]
[Channel subscription reminder]
```

## 2. YOUTUBE SHORTS TEMPLATES

### 🔥 TRENDING SOUND / VIRAL HOOK

```markdown
# 🎬 YOUTUBE SHORT: HOOK + VALUE

**Duration:** 25-55 seconds
**Format:** Hook → Content → CTA

---

## HOOK OPTIONS:

Option A: "POV: You finally understand [topic]..."
Option B: "Wait, you didn't know this about [topic]?"
Option C: "3 things about [topic] that changed my life:"
Option D: "The problem with [common thing] is..."
Option E: "Stop doing [X] if you want [Y]"

---

## TEMPLATE:

### (0:00 - 0:03) HOOK
[Eye contact, energetic, point at camera]

"[HOOK TEXT]"

---

### (0:03 - 0:20) CONTENT
[Main content - fast paced]

"[Content point 1]"

"[Content point 2]"

"[Content point 3]"

---

### (0:20 - 0:30) CTA
[Point at screen]

"Follow for more [topic] tips"

---

## TECHNICAL TIPS:
- Use trending audio (check YouTube trending sounds)
- Vertical video (9:16 ratio)
- Bright, contrasting colors
- Text overlays for key points
- Fast cuts keep attention
```

---

### 🎓 QUICK TIPS SHORTS

```markdown
# 🎬 YOUTUBE SHORT: QUICK TIPS

**Duration:** 30-60 seconds
**Format:** Number reveal + Quick tip

---

## TEMPLATE:

### (0:00 - 0:05) HOOK
[Fast cut, energetic]

"X tips for [topic] in X seconds:"

---

"1. [Tip 1]"
"2. [Tip 2]"
"3. [Tip 3]"
```

## 4. THUMBNAIL TEMPLATES

### 🎨 HIGH-CTR THUMBNAIL FORMULA

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                     │
│  ┌─────┐                           │
│  │     │  ← FACE/EXPRESSION        │
│  │ 😊  │    (Close up, emotional)  │
│  │     │                           │
│  └─────┘                           │
│                                     │
│  ┌─────────────────────────────┐   │
│  │   MAIN TEXT (Large, Bold)   │   │
│  │   [HOOK TEXT IN CAPS]       │   │
│  └─────────────────────────────┘   │
│                                     │
│  ┌───────────────────────────┐      │
│  │  SUBTEXT (Smaller)       │      │
│  │  [Supporting detail]     │      │
│  └───────────────────────────┘      │
│                                     │
│  [Extra visual element: arrows,     │
│   circles, explosion effects]       │
│                                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 📐 THUMBNAIL SPECS:
- **Resolution:** 1280x720 (16:9)
- **File size:** Under 2MB
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Text:** Large, bold, readable at small size
- **Colors:** High contrast, bright
- **Face:** Include human face (even if cropped)
- **Expression:** Emotion - surprised, excited, concerned

### 🎭 EMOTION MAPPING:

| Goal | Face Expression | Text Example |
|------|----------------|---------------|
| Curiosity | Surprised/shocked | "Wait, what?" |
| Benefit | Excited/happy | "I finally did it!" |
| Warning | Concerned/worried | "Stop doing this" |
| Comparison | Curious/interested | "X vs Y" |
| Tutorial | Confident/focused | "How to do X" |

### 🎯 GROWTH HACKS

**Quick Wins:**
1. Upload 3 Shorts/week (huge reach boost)
2. Reply to every comment (engagement)
3. End screen on every video
4. Collab with similar-sized channels
5. Upload at peak times (test your audience)

**Algorithm Tips:**
1. Retention > Views (focus on watch time)
2. CTR > Thumbnail (make better thumbnails)
3. Comments > Likes (encourage discussion)
4. Shares > Subscribers (create shareable content)
