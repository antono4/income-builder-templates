## ============================================
## 💡 DATA-DRIVEN BUSINESS TIPS:
## ============================================
1. ✅ Always validate assumptions with data
2. ✅ Track metrics consistently over time
3. ✅ Look at trends, not just point-in-time data
4. ✅ Compare against industry benchmarks
5. ✅ Use data to tell a story, not just report
6. ✅ Share insights with team regularly
7. ✅ Make data accessible and actionable
8. ❌ Don't cherry-pick data to support biases
9. ❌ Don't ignore qualitative data
10. ❌ Don't make decisions based on one data point
## ============================================
